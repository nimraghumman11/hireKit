Software engineering salary benchmarks (US market, 2024): Junior SWE: $80,000–$120,000 base. Mid SWE: $120,000–$160,000 base. Senior SWE: $160,000–$220,000 base. Staff / Lead SWE: $200,000–$280,000 base. Principal / Director Eng: $250,000–$400,000+ base. Total compensation (base + equity + bonus) often 1.3x–2x base at growth-stage and public companies. Remote-first companies typically use national or tiered geo rates.
