Engineering competency framework — levels: L1 Junior: Completes well-scoped tasks with guidance. Writes tests. Communicates blockers. L2 Mid: Ships features independently. Reviews others' code. Estimates accurately. L3 Senior: Designs systems. Mentors L1-L2. Influences team processes. L4 Staff/Lead: Sets technical direction. Solves cross-team problems. Grows engineers. L5 Principal: Org-wide technical strategy. Industry-recognized expertise. Drives culture.
