Database interview topics: ACID properties and when to trade them off. Indexing strategies: B-tree vs. hash, composite indexes, partial indexes, covering indexes. Query optimization: EXPLAIN, avoiding N+1, index scans vs. seq scans. Normalization vs. denormalization trade-offs. Transactions and isolation levels (READ COMMITTED, REPEATABLE READ, SERIALIZABLE). Sharding, replication (primary-replica, multi-region), connection pooling. When to use a relational vs. document vs. time-series vs. graph database.
