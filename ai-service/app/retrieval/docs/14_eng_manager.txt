Engineering Manager responsibilities: Manages a team of 4-10 engineers: hiring, onboarding, performance management, and growth. Ensures team delivery against roadmap commitments with high quality and predictability. Removes blockers and shields the team from organizational noise. Conducts regular 1:1s focused on career development and well-being. Drives engineering culture: psychological safety, blameless post-mortems, continuous improvement. Partners with Product and Design leadership to align on priorities.
